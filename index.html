About

During Fallas, receive a daily notification featuring the monument of the day. Discover its location and history, walk through the city and upload a photo to preserve your memories of the celebration.

How it works

1
Receive the daily Fallas monument notification.
2
Walk to its location and discover its story.
3
Upload a photo to save your memory of the day.
4
Build your own collection throughout the celebration.

Explore the city

Firemap turns every day of Fallas into a new route. Find the monument on the map, learn its history, and enjoy Valencia by walking from one discovery to the next.

Tip: use Firemap as your daily plan — one monument, one walk, one memory.
